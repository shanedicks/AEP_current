Testing

You must test before beginning classes.

CCR Students take the TABE test

  • This test normally lasts around 2 hours, depending on how quickly you read & move through it.
  • The TABE test is done via computer.
    • There is a paper-based version, but students wanting to test that way will need to file an Accommodations Request.
    • There are audio, read-aloud directions available for the computer based test that can also be assigned after filing an Accommodations Request.
  • The TABE test has three sections
    • Math - part 1
    • Math - part 2
    • Language

ESL Students take the CLAS-E test

  • This test normally lasts between 1-2 hours, depending on how quickly you read & move through it.
  • The CLAS-E test is done via computer & paper
  • The CLAS-E test has one mandatory sections
    • Reading

It is expected for students who successfully complete an 8 week session to also test at the end of it.

Having up-to-date test scores is what we use to determine what classes you should take as you move through the program.

Accommodations

If you would like to request accommodations for testing or classes, please tell your Intake Advisor!
