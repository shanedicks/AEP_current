CCR

College and Career Readiness

Your journey in our program, depending on your interests, might lead you towards CCR classes.

Our CCR Classes are designed to prepare you in Math, Reading, & Writing for several different goals.

Your goals might include:

  • studying for the HiSET exam & high school equivalency (formerly GED)
  • preparing for college, or college placement exams
  • simply brushing up on your skills!

Classes and Sessions

DCCAEP is a free program! We do not offer credit-earning courses, so none of our classes have a fee.

You will schedule classes based on your placement scores from the TABE test and what growth-areas the assessment tells us you have-- accommodations for testing & classes are available for students.

We offer classes in a variety of subjects, such as:

  • Algebra
  • Narrative Writing
  • HiSET Science, and more

Sessions generally last for 8 weeks, and you can take as few or as many classes as you like. You can repeat a class until you feel you have mastered it, and there is no limit to the number of sessions you can attend.


Once you are registered with DCCAEP, you never have to re-register again! Even if you need to take a break for awhile, you do not need to re-register. You can just talk to the Main Office or make a coaching appointment to get back in.


We offer CCR classes both online & in-person! Please keep in mind that no children or guests are allowed for our in-person classes, so you may want to consider that when choosing which mode of instruction works best with your life and schedule.

HiSET? Highschool Equivalency? GED?


HiSET Exam

In 2014 Louisiana switched from using the GED exam to the HiSET exam.

The HiSET exam is a test someone can take in order to prove they have the same knowledge-level as someone who graduated from highschool. All colleges & universities will accept a HiSET diploma the same way they would accept a highschool diploma-- that is what makes it a highschool equivalency.

Is this the test you take to get into the Adult Education Program?

No, the HiSET is different from the placement test you take with us-- the test that we use for placement into CCR classes is the Test of Adult Basic Education -- TABE test. The TABE lets us know where you are in your Math, Reading, and Writing skills.

We can however help guide you to Practice HiSET exams for students to use to check their own progress.
