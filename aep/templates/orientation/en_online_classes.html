Online Classes

What can I expect from online classes?

When you enroll in our program, you will be assigned classes that focus on particular skills within a subject area. These classes consist of 7 lessons and run for 8 weeks.


All classes are delivered through Google classroom, and you will be able to access them on any computer with an internet connection, or through the Google classroom app downloaded on any mobile device.


You will get individualized feedback from an instructor, who will provide support, engage with your work, and link you to further resources.


You can work through lessons as they post every week at your own pace, but there are flexible weekly deadlines to keep you on track and give you timely feedback. Additionally, there are coaches who can check-in with you to help guide you throughout the session.


What are my next steps after this orientation?

First, you will still need to schedule a testing appointment!

After testing, you will be booked for another appointment with your Intake Advisor. During this appointment, they will email you with instructions and login information for setting up your Google account, which you will use to access your classes in Google classroom.


Your new Google account will be an email address with your full name @elearnclass.org. So an example for President Obama would be: barack.obama@elearnclass.org


Even if you have an existing Gmail account, we will need for you to use this new email account for schoolwork and classes.
