In-Person Classes

When are the classes?

CCR Classes

Classes last for 1.5 (one & a half) hours.

  • example: 8:30-10:00 AM
  • example: 5:30-7:00 PM

Classes are held twice a week on either:

  • Monday & Wednesday

OR

  • Tuesday & Thursday

There is no option for different combinations

  • example: there are no classes that are Monday & Tuesday


ESL Classes

Classes last for 3 (three) hours.

  • example: 8:30-11:30 AM
  • example: 5:30-8:30 PM

Classes are held twice a week on either:

  • Monday & Wednesday

OR

  • Tuesday & Thursday

There is no option for different combinations

  • example: there are no classes that are Monday & Tuesday


Attendance

Both ESL & CCR in-person classes have a maximum number of absences -- 4 absences are allowed, on the 5th absence you will be dropped from the class. This is NOT the same thing as being dropped from the program! You will still have access to coaching appointments, study labs, and any classes you have not been dropped from.

Even if you are dropped from a class, you can always sign up for new classes in the next session! Once you finish your New Student Process, you will never need to re-register.


Children & Guests

Not allowed. This is a liability issue for us-- the only people who are allowed in our classes are the folks who are on the class roster. You are not allowed to bring anyone with you to class who is not a fellow student.
